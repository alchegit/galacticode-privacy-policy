Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

Galacticode ("the app") does not collect or store any personal information from users. This app operates offline and does not request, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII).

1. Information We Collect

No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by this app.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Not applicable. This app does not access or send any user data to external servers.

3. Data Storage and Deletion

All data (e.g., galactic languages and messages) are stored locally on the user’s device and are deleted when the app is uninstalled.

4. Third-Party Sharing

This app does not share any information with third parties under any circumstances.

This app uses the Google AdMob advertising SDK to display ads. Google may collect data such as device information, advertising ID, and usage patterns for the purpose of serving more relevant and higher-quality ads. This data does not personally identify users and is used solely to improve ad experiences.
